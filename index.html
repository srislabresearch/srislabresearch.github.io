About the Lab

The Secure Reliable Intelligent Systems (SRIS) Lab The research lab, led by a prominent investigator with expertise in Data Management and Analytics, Security and Privacy, Edge Computing, Blockchain, and the Internet of Things, has garnered significant acclaim and funding. As either a principal investigator (PI) or co-PI, the lead researcher has successfully secured over $5 million from diverse sources such as NSF, NASA, and various universities. Projects have focused on innovative applications like robust multi-robot systems in dynamic environments and blockchain for system engineering. The lab's work spans practical and theoretical aspects, aiming to push the boundaries of how data and technology can be utilized in dynamic, real-world scenarios.
